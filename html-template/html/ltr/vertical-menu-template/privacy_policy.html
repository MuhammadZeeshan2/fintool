Privacy and Policy

Introduction

At Image Processing System, we are committed to safeguarding the privacy and security of our user's data. This Privacy and Policy document outlines how we collect, use, process, and protect the information and images you upload to our Image Processing System, especially when using Google Drive as an upload source.

Information Collection and Use

When you use our Image Processing System, you may be asked to provide certain personal information for identification purposes or to enhance your user experience. This information may include, but is not limited to, your name, email address, and any images or content you choose to upload through Google Drive.

Google Drive Uploads

Our system allows users to upload images directly from Google Drive for processing. We understand the importance of these images to you and assure you of the following:

  • Security: Our application resides in a highly secure environment designed to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We employ a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information.
  • Privacy: The images or any content you upload through Google Drive are used solely for the purpose you intend within our Image Processing System. We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your uploaded content with third parties for their promotional purposes.
  • Control: You retain full control over your images and content. At any point, you may request the deletion of your data from our system, subject to our data retention policies.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your uploaded images or personal information with third parties, except as necessary to provide the services requested or when required by law. In cases where we must share data for legal reasons, we will ensure that the minimal necessary information is disclosed.

Data Security

The security of your personal information and uploaded content is paramount to us. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access or use of your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Image Processing System reserves the right to update or change our Privacy and Policy at any time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy and Policy on this page or through other means of communication. Your continued use of our service after any modifications to the Privacy and Policy will constitute your acknowledgment of the modifications and your consent to abide and be bound by the modified Privacy and Policy.
